Body Mass Index (BMI) is a numerical value derived from an individual's weight and height. This is calculated by dividing someone’s weight in kilograms by the square of their height in meters. It is used as a tool to categorize individuals into various weight categories such as underweight and obesity. BMI is a useful and simple measure for considering weight-related health risks for someone’s vitality and will to live.
